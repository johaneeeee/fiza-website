About Fiza Events

Professional Event Support & Resource Partner

Who We Are

We specialize in creating professional business events that bring together decision-makers, investors, entrepreneurs, government representatives, and industry experts.

Through carefully curated conferences, networking forums, investment summits, and executive roundtables, we create platforms where ideas become partnerships and opportunities become investments.

Our main focus is to be a trusted resource and event support company, providing staffing, event operations, logistics, speakers, sponsorship management, marketing, and more.

Our flagship initiative in the coming years will be to launch a major Investment Summit, positioning Mauritius as a premier destination for investment, innovation, and sustainable business growth across Africa and the Indian Ocean region.

Our Mission

To deliver seamless, high-quality event support and resources that help our clients create meaningful business connections and successful outcomes.

Our Vision

To become the leading event support partner and, in the near future, successfully launch one of the most prestigious investment summits in the Indian Ocean region.
